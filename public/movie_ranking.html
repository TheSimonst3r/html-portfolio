The Best Movies According to Simone

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Interstellar

Speaks to my interest in astrophysics, a wondrous marvel of visual effects.

The Unbearable Weight of Massive Talent

Nicholas Cage's best performance in my opinion, extremely meta and hilarious.

The Grand Budapest Hotel

One of my favorite Wes Anderson flicks; the dry humour pulled me into the captivating and convoluted plot immediately.
